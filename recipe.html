MoiMoi/Beans Pudding

Special Delicacy Perfect for family Gatherings and Special Occasions.

Nutritional Facts

  • Calories: 200 per serving
  • Protein: 10g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fat: 5g
  • Fiber: 4g

Recipe Information

Preparation Time: 1 hour

Cooking Time: 45 minutes

Total Time: 1 hour 45 minutes

Servings: 6-8 servings

Ingredients

  • Peeled beans (70%)
  • Coconut Milk
  • Curry Powder
  • Moimoi Leaves
  • 2-3 tablespoons of ground crayfish
  • Pepper mix (Tatase-10%,Bawa-8%,habenro pepper 2%,onions 15%)
  • Salt to taste
  • Vegetable oil (optional)
  • Water (as needed)
  • Maggi to taste
  • Titus fish (optional)
  • Egg
  • Any Fillings of choice

Instructions

  1. Soak your beans for 20 minutes and wash your beans very clean.
  2. Use enough onions,bawa,tatase.
  3. Blend the beans with the pepper mix and add coconut milk.
  4. Add curry powder, ground crayfish, salt, and Maggi to the mixture.
  5. Mix thoroughly until smooth,Preferably use a whisk to mix it properly
  6. Wash moi moi leaves thoroughly
  7. Place the washed moi moi leaves in a bowl or moi moi pot.
  8. Add deboned titus in to the mixture
  9. Add vegetable oil,mix it well
  10. Pour the mixture into the moi moi leaves, filling them about 3/4 full.
  11. Add your egg and any fillings of choice.
  12. Place the wraps in the pot
  13. Cover your moi moi with some leaves and add hot water on it
  14. Cook for 45 minutes
  15. Check if it is done by inserting a toothpick or knife into the moi moi, it should come out clean.
  16. Once done, remove from heat and allow to cool slightly before serving.

Tips

  • Ensure the beans are well peeled for a smooth texture.
  • Adjust the pepper mix according to your spice preference.
  • For a richer flavor, you can add more coconut milk.
  • Experiment with different fillings like vegetables or meats.
  • Add Raw egg for for a great texture

Enjoy your delicious MoiMoi!
